# Safety Checklist

Use this checklist before shipping features that affect matching, messaging, profiles, or visibility.

## User protection

- Can users block and report when needed?
- Does this feature expose more identity or location data than necessary?
- Could blocked users still appear or interact unexpectedly?
- Are consent and visibility choices clear?

## Abuse prevention

- Could this increase spam, harassment, impersonation, or ban evasion?
- Are there rate limits, review hooks, or eligibility checks where needed?
- Could users exploit this feature to contact people who opted out?

## Privacy

- Is data collection minimized?
- Are defaults conservative?
- Are sensitive fields protected from unnecessary logs or analytics?

## UX

- Are safety actions understandable and easy to find?
- Are messages calm, respectful, and specific?
- Does the feature preserve trust even in edge cases?
