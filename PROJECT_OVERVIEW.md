# Flint Project Overview

## Product summary

Flint is a mobile dating app for iOS and Android focused on trust, profile quality, fast matching, and respectful communication.

## Product goals

- Shorten time from signup to first meaningful match.
- Make onboarding lightweight but high signal.
- Build user trust through strong privacy and safety patterns.
- Support healthy conversations instead of spammy engagement loops.

## Target experience

- Clean, modern, mobile-first design.
- Low-friction onboarding.
- Clear profile prompts and matching flows.
- Safety controls that are easy to access when needed.

## Core domains

- Authentication and account lifecycle.
- Profile creation and editing.
- Matching, ranking, and eligibility.
- Messaging and conversation starts.
- Moderation, reporting, blocking, and privacy.
- Subscriptions and premium features.

## Engineering posture

- Prefer predictable architecture over clever abstractions.
- Favor additive, reversible changes.
- Protect auth, moderation, data, and billing paths.
